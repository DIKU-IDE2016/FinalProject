Cancer in the US

In the United states, the number of deaths by cancer have been on the rise over the past few years.

What are the trends in the development of cancers over the past few years in the United States ?


On this webpage, you will see a sequence of visualizations that will show you the trends in the development of the various cancers affecting men and women over the years. All the data used are derived from SEER program


To see the first visualizaion scroll down or click on the bouncing arrow below.

Leading Causes of Death in US, 1975 vs 2013

     As seen in this pie chart (hover over sections to compare them), the percentage of death causes for all maligant cancer has grown significantly in time period from 1975 to 2013. It has risen from 19.3% to 22.6% - growth of 3.3%. Meanwhile, Pneumonia, accidents, cerebrovascular diseases and heart disease have reduced percentage-wise, but other causes and chronic lung diseases have increased as well. Cancer is becoming more and more common thoughout America and people need to be aware of the types of cancer and how to prevent or treat them.
